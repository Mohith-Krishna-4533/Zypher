Cybersecurity Tips

Stay smart. Stay secure.

Use Strong Passwords

Avoid names or birthdays. Use a mix of characters and enable password managers.

Beware of Phishing

Never click unknown links. Verify the sender before opening any attachment.

Keep Software Updated

Updates often contain critical security patches. Always stay up to date.

Avoid Public Wi-Fi

Use VPNs when on open networks. Avoid banking or shopping on public Wi-Fi.

Enable 2FA

Two-Factor Authentication adds another layer of protection to your accounts.
